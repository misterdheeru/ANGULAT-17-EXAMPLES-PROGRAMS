DATA SCIENCE

Data science is the study of data to extract meaningful insights for business and industry. It uses a multidisciplinary approach that combines principles and practices from fields like mathematics, statistics, artificial intelligence, and computer engineering to analyze large amounts of data. Data scientists use tools, methods, and technology such as data analysis and modeling, human-machine interaction, and algorithms to ask and answer questions like: What happened, Why did it happen, What will happen, and How can the results be used for planning and decision-making.
